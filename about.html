Empowering Women and Girls

A native of St. Louis, MO., Brandy Cruesoe has been serving her community as a licensed therapist since 2008. Since 2005 Brandy has worked in a range of behavioral health settings. Her experience includes working with acute mental illness in adults; adult and teen survivors of sexual assault/abuse; families struggling with parent/teen dynamics. Additionally, Brandy has experience facilitating group topics including the following:

Self Reclamation Improving Self-Esteem Healthy Relationships Body Image
Peer Pressure Goal Setting and Planning Stree Management Parental Support

Brandy holds a Bachelor of Arts (BA) degree in Psychology as well as a Master of Social Work (MSW) degree. Her official clinical credentialing is Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW). She is currently pursuing certification as a Certified Sex Therapist (CST) with the American Association of Sexuality Educators, Counselors, and Therapists (AASECT), and holds active membership within this organization. Brandy is dedicated to bringing forth and supporting the best in clients so they can live more fruitful lives.
