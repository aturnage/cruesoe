Life can be demanding at times and these demands can create stress, dysfunction, and unrest. Whether you’re a teen girl dealing with cliques and self esteem, a woman dealing with life struggles or a couple struggling with communication differences, help is available.

My focus is to help the everyday person: the woman struggling to raise her kids or reinvent herself; the woman trying to find balance in work/life demands; the woman trying to heal from trauma and abuse; the teen girl trying to find her way and navigate through life and school or with friends; the couple that doesn’t seem to be on the same page in and out of the bedroom. My goal is to help you improve these and other areas of your life. Together we can build upon your strengths, improve what’s not working, and move towards healing and wholeness.

As a client, you can gain insight to equip yourself to make necessary and beneficial changes, while drawing from natural supports to create new, healthy adaptive habits and resources. I am here to help support those who are motivated and ready to learn and grow, and make healthy changes in their lives. My philosophy is to live your best life and I want to help you do exactly that.
